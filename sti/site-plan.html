About us

Soporte Técnico Invernadero is a small entrepreneurial business that began on December 30, 2020, with the aim of providing computer services to the community.

What we offer?

We offer a variety of services oriented to desktop computers and laptops, such as: diagnostics, preventive and corrective maintenance, PC assemblies, information backups, file recovery, sale of win10 and win11 licenses. We also have a web page development service oriented to small businesses.

What is preventive maintenance?

It is a preventive cleaning maintenance inside and outside of a computer or laptop. Nowadays, most of us have a portable device, be it a cell phone, a laptop, or a desktop computer at home. If we are careless and place these components in inappropriate places, over time they will accumulate dust, adhere to the electronic cards, and in the long run different problems will arise, be they intermittent, performance and that the computer does not turn on. So that this does not start, it is essential to maintain it every 6 months, we offer you a thorough and in-depth service to extract dust and other residues inside the cabinet of your computer or laptop.

I have an old hard disk, do you backup files from old hard drives with IDE and PATA interface?

Of course, as long as the hard disk does not have physical and mechanical damaged.

Do you assemble computer equipment?

Sure, we can build the equipment you want according to your budget, be it a computer for basic tasks to a gaming computer.

Do you have home services?

Not actually, but we have equipment collection service at home with an additional cost.
